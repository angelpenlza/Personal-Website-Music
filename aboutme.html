About Me

School

I am a computer science major at CSUF. Immediately after highschool, I went to Fullerton Community College, both because I wanted to save money and because I wasn't sure what I wanted to pursue as a major. I started as an electrical engineer major, but switched my major to computer science after a year. I enjoy this field significantly more, although it is just as, if not even more difficult than the EE path. In my EE path, I had to take a computer science class as a requirement. I had a friend who was also majoring in CS, and he was struggling with it despite being much more intelligent than I was. Because of this, I decided to study for the class during the summer so that the class wasn't so heavy on me when I took it. I ended up having a lot of fun getting simple programs to run and saw the possible careers that a computer science degree could lead to, which seemed much more appealing than the electrical engineering jobs.

Work

Currently, I work the in the tech area of Target. I've gone through various jobs, all pretty unique from one another. Aside from Target, I've also worked at Northgate and Raising Canes. I didn't like either of those two jobs, and Target, although I had a rough start, is probably the best of the three. I am hoping to get a job at CSUF because I believe that it will be easier to manage.
